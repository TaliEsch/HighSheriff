There are two options:

You can either choose to fill out the written form, or you can submit a video application.
For the video application, please use the questions given below. Some questions have
suggested time limits, but these do not have to be strictly adhered to.

  • What is the name of your charity?
  • What is your charity involved with? (2 mins)
  • Why do you think your charity should be given funding? (2-5 mins)
  • Where is your charity based?
  • How many people are working/volunteering for your charity currently?
